Chicken Carbonara

Description

Chicken carbonara is a creamy, indulgent Italian-inspired dish that combines tender slices of grilled or pan-seared chicken breast with rich, velvety pasta. The sauce is made from a blend of eggs, grated Parmesan cheese, and a hint of garlic, creating a luscious texture that clings to every strand of pasta. Crispy pancetta or bacon adds a savory crunch, while freshly cracked black pepper enhances the depth of flavor. Each bite offers a delightful balance of creamy and salty, with the chicken adding a hearty protein element that makes it perfect for a comforting meal. Garnished with fresh parsley, it’s both satisfying and elegant.

Ingredients

  • Pancetta
  • Spaghetti
  • Egg Yolks
  • Parmesan
  • Heavy Cream
  • Chicken
  • Parsley

Steps

  1. Cook the pancetta until lightly bornwed and crisp, the set aside.
  2. Boil spaghetti, reserve two cups of pasta water, and set aside.
  3. Whisk the eggs in a bowl, then whisk in 1 ½ cups of the hot pasta water.
  4. Whisk in the cheese and cream.
  5. Add the pasta, pancetta, and chicken to the sauce and toss.
  6. Transfer the mixture to a prepared baking dish and bake until the sauce is thickened.
  7. Stir and let stand so the sauce can continue to thicken. Add more pasta water if you like.
  8. Garnish with parsley and Parmesan cheese.
